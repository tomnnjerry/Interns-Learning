To get you started, here are 5 proven tips to reduce DSO in 2025:
 
  1. Set Clear Payment Terms Upfront
     
    Transparent payment terms prevent confusion and set the foundation for on-time payments.
     
  2. Invoice Promptly and Accurately
     
    Errors or delays in invoicing are a leading cause of late payments. Accurate, timely invoices can make a world of difference.
     
  3. Automate Your Follow-Ups
     
    Automated reminders ensure you stay top-of-mind without straining your resources.
     
  4. Offer Flexible Payment Options
     
    Customers are more likely to pay on time when you offer convenient payment methods like credit cards, ACH, or online portals.
     
  5. Monitor Aging Reports Regularly
     
    Keeping tabs on overdue accounts helps you prioritize and recover payments faster.
