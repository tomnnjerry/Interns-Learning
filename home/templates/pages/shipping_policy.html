Shipping Policy
What are the shipping options available?

We offer the following shipping options for your convenience:

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within [5-7 business days] for domestic orders.
  • Express Shipping: Delivery within [2-3 business days] for an additional fee.
  • International Shipping: Delivery timelines vary based on location, typically [7-14 business days].
What are the shipping charges?

- Domestic Orders: Free shipping on orders above ₹10,000. A flat rate of ₹200 applies for orders below this amount.
- International Orders: Shipping charges are calculated based on the destination and weight of the package, visible at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking link via email. You can use this link to monitor your package's progress in real-time. Alternatively, you can log in to your account on our website and view the order tracking details under "My Orders."

What is the estimated delivery time?

- Domestic Orders: Standard shipping takes [5-7 business days], and express shipping takes [2-3 business days].
- International Orders: Delivery timelines vary, typically ranging from [7-14 business days], depending on the destination.

Do you ship to multiple addresses?

Currently, we can only ship to one address per order. If you would like to ship to multiple addresses, please place separate orders for each destination.

What happens if my package is delayed?

While we strive to deliver your orders on time, unforeseen circumstances like weather conditions or courier delays may affect delivery times. If your package is delayed, please contact our customer service team at [support@koxari.com], and we will assist you promptly.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to select international destinations. International shipping charges and delivery timelines are calculated at checkout. Please note that customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties or import taxes in their country.
